How It Works

A simple flow from posting to hiring

SmartTask Connect keeps the hiring process clear for both task owners and workers.

1

Post a Task

Task owners add task details, budget, location, date, and required skills.

2

Match by Skills

The system compares worker skills with task requirements and shows a match score.

3

Invite or Apply

Owners can invite matched workers, and workers can accept, reject, or apply to tasks.

4

Chat and Complete

After acceptance, chat becomes available and the task can be completed and reviewed.
